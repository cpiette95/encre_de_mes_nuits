Night Chat with the Chameleon Moon

April 5, 2026
Do you prefer my smoky camouflage or my smirking smile? Well, we'd rather watch your gothic blood-red hue or devilish spikes. All year long, I want to play hide-and-seek. Ugh. Game on! There will be no rest under my light. Will you dare follow my rules, alright? We, careless humans, barely watch your sweep. Mmmh… lights on! We are chasing you tonight. How does it feel, finally being in the spotlight?
· · ·

Context & Notes

Written the night when Artemis II was launched to orbit around the Moon, "chasing after her" once again...
