Absence

June 15, 2024
Tainted wine meandering through my body, dying blood dripping off my leg, poisoned peanuts stuck in my stomach, waiting for… with tears, tears, tears, tearing me apart from you … the unbearable pending End?
· · ·

Context & Notes

A night suspended in expectation of a message that never came, with wine and peanuts keeping company to my darkest thoughts.
