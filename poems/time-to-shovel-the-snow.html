Time to shovel the snow

January 7, 2026
A week ago it was apparently time to shovel the snow. A future was irrevocably lost, cut short — like a precious hair tie swept away by water Call it a miracle if it ever comes ashore salt-stung, frayed, and still treasured as ever. Unlike the untouched, endlessly returning snow — probably time again to shovel it now.
· · ·

Context & Notes

"Shoveling the snow": an English expression I learned during the winter of 2025–2026, an activity R. repeatedly undertook during the snowstorms that winter, and one that became inseparable from the memory of our break-up.
