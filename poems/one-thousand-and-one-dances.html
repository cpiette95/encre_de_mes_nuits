One Thousand and One Dances

May 21, 2026
Timeless months in exuberant imagination, unstoppable hours of breathless life, and revolving days trapped in memory. All at the dear palace of my Nir-avana. Watching — a disco moon and its constellations of static stars and passing guests. And there I meet you: eye-to-eye, foot-to-foot, ready to tell a new tale to the night. I let you walk me, drop me, lift me, spin me up and down. Now let me feel your edges: like a string hanging on a cliff ledge, a top trembling at the rim of a crystal, a needle skimming an overinflated balloon. Let me feel the elasticity, the suspension, the vertigo — don't let it go. Only then will my toes be grounded on the floor when the music stops Only then will my body be still and strong when the movement ends Yet, no matter what — I will lie in shreds when pressed against your chest.
