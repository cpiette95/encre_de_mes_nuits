Invade me

May 12, 2026
We are sitting next to each other: a solitude of white ink, a silence of black feathers. That blank page could be wept in cries, that bare pillow could be filled with howls. Talk to me, invade me. We are waiting, our dear Time passing. Let Him get mad Let Him turn bad. As we shall die, no longer stranger in each other's eyes.
· · ·

Context & Notes

A poem about exploring the different parts of oneself and the difficulty they sometimes have in accepting one another.
