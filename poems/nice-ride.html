It Was a Nice Ride

June 16, 2024
to Pinsky's Savior
It was a nice ride Mi hermoso maestro Repetido, repetido, repetido This course is all about writing In the disciplines. Not forgetting Structure, language, and reference styl-ing. It was a nice ride Yet once the gravy hour dings Doody dandy duty springs Comes arros, aguacates y acai Pero, por favor, no thyme and no curry For mi dulce grizzly. It was a nice ride Mi muñeca linda, viva la Bohemia From Comayagua to Tegucigalpa Oh to Paris, let's go now! S'enivrer d'une idylle comme Artaud 'T is too my dearest vow bro! It was a nice ride Driving and biking together from Havana After our spinning bachata Electrical sparks to chase Like your soft hands around my waist Before us dying in wild embrace. It was a nice ride Till dawn, when the robin starts singing And I can still hear your whisper longing "Oh, fuck...". Mi Romeo, Are you home now? 'Cause I sleep like a cow…
