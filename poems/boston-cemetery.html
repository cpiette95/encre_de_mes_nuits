Today I applied to Boston's cemetery

April 5, 2026
Today I applied to Boston's cemetery Resident permit parking only. Estimated waiting time: until next January. Death will keep in touch. She says, no rush! Don't be a fool. Keep to the rules: No dancing and singing in the bright. Stay underground until twilight. Special guests allowed at night.
· · ·

Context & Notes

The origin of the poem: a slip of the tongue in a conversation at night about a friend's applying to the "seminary", which I called the "cemetery". From there, an inner conversation with Death starts...
