Resident Permit

April 5, 2026
Today I applied to Boston's cemetery Resident permit parking only. Estimated waiting time: until next January. Death will keep in touch. She says, no rush! Don't be a fool. Keep to the rules: No dancing and singing in the bright / under daylight. Stay underground until twilight. Special guests allowed at night.
