No longer aligned

May 7, 2026
This time, the wind from heavens did not propel any waves. Silence in the clouds, silence on the earth. No longer aligned, no longer loving or fighting, two hearts keep beating. Beating to the music of their separate dances. Beating to the moment-to-moment vibrations of their separate lives. But arrhythmic hearts never follow a single beat – so why do you not keep caring?
