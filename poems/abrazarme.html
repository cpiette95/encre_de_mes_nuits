Abrazarme

January 3, 2026
The gentle breeze this evening Tinkles against my earrings, And I, free-floating Cannot but embrace his wings. Ahí te conocí. Then, the wind turned cold. Burnt my lips, raked my skin. Como un fósforo raspado contra abrasivo. I curled into a shell, layer upon layer until no breath could enter. The icy blasts would not shatter But they still struck like a hammer. Duele, y callé.
