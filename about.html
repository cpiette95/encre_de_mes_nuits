About

I began writing poetry regularly about two years ago, shortly after leaving Paris and moving to Boston for my postdoctoral studies in neuroscience. Since then, serendipitous encounters quickly became sources of inspiration amidst the rhythms of science, biking, and dancing.

Three languages inhabit my writing and reading. French, my mother tongue. English, which I was first introduced to through intensive British and American literature & history classes in high school, and which I later used in my scientific path. Spanish, discovered in 2023 through a short period of life shared with Argentinians, and then cultivated in Boston thanks to my « Latino » friends and the readings of Hispanic authors.

A special shout-out to Friday Night Open Mic at Little Crêpe Café in Cambridge (citynightreadings.com), where I first started sharing some of these poems.

Charlotte Piette
